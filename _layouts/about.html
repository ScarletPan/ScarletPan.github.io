Haojie Pan

Currently, I am an algorithm expert at Kuaishou Technology, leading the project Kuaipedia , which is the first large-scale multi-modal short-video encyclopedia in the world. Before that, I am an senior algorithm engineer at Alibaba Cloud, developing large scale NLP toolkits on PAI . I got my Mphil degree from HKUST, supervised by Prof. Yangqiu Song. I received my B.E. degree at ZJU in June 2018, supervised by Prof. Deng Cai. I have worked in Tencent Wechat group, Alibaba DAMO Academy NLP Group and Netease NetEase Fuxi AI Lab.

I work on text mining, natural language processing and knowledge graph.
