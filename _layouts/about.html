Haojie Pan

I am a senior algorithm engineer at Alibaba Cloud. Before that, I got my Mphil degree from HKUST, supervised by Prof. Yangqiu Song. I received my B.E. degree at ZJU in June 2018, supervised by Prof. Deng Cai. I have worked in Netease NetEase Fuxi AI Lab, Alibaba DAMO Academy NLP Group and Tencent Wechat group.

I work on text mining, natural language processing and machine learning.
